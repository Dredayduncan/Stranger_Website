Stranger danger.

At Don't Be A Stranger, we understand the vital role that brands play today. Brands occupy space in people's everyday lives, and are able to influence their everyday choices.

We believe people navigate the world today, through brands.

That's why our work focuses on helping brands in three ways:

1. Relatability

Identifying the truth of a brand, and sharing it in ways that an audience can relate and connect with, to drive sales.

2. Relevance

Elevating a brand from the commercial (selling products), to the meaningful (adding value to people by selling products).

3. Interest

Designing tools that capture - and reward - the attention and imagination of a brand's audience.

Meet the founder.

Jean is fast becoming Ghana's most internationally awarded art director and creative individual. Since breaking onto the design and advertising scene in 2019, Jean's work has been recognized with some of the highest industry honors, such as Graphis Awards (1 Gold, 2 Silver), Communication Arts Awards (1 Gold), Creativity International Awards (1 Bronze), AD Stars (1 Finalist), and 3 Finalist Honors from The Loerie Awards; the highest creative accolade in Africa and Middle East. His work has also been featured in online publications including Ads of the World, DesignTAXI, Creative Bloq and Brilliant Ads.

With a background in art direction and copywriting for advertising, Jean has lent his relentless passion for creative excellence to clients like AirtelTigo, Shell, Total, Surfline, Club Beer, Devtraco Plus, Vanguard Assurance, MiLife Insurance, National Road Safety Commission and Ideal Milk, as well as successful collaborations with Ashesi University, The United Nations, Pantone Color Institute and Designers Against Coronavirus (DAC).

Jean has now shifted his creative appetite to brand design, with a special focus on rebranding. To that end, he has founded Don't Be A Stranger: a design agency that exists to design businesses better, so they can make people better.
